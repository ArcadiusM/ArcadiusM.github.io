Main goal of the project

The aim of the project was to analyse some basic data related to two capital cities Warsaw and Berlin. Similarities in these two capital cities was checked to find patterns between venues which are located there. Comparison of these two capitals can help people who are looking for answers related to similarities in these places. It is also valuable for people who are curious and want to know places which are located in Warsaw and Berlin.

For this project data available on the Foursquare's API was used. It was used to check data in Berlin and Warsaw in terms of their neighborhoods. The data used includes information about different venues and their neighborhoods. Moreover data about districts of these two cities, which was available on wikipedia.com, was used.
